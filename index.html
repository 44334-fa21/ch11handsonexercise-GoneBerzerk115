5 Helpful Facts About Copyright Law and the Web

Facts that can save your wallet!

  • Fact 1: Images

    - Images whose copyright protection has expired are considered "Public Domain" and can be freely used by anybody. Source

  • Fact 2: Length of Copyrights

    - A person's copyrighted work is protected for life and 70 years after death in the US. Source

  • Fact 3: Limits to what can be copyright

    - Just about everything can be protected under a copyright if it is a tangle thing. Ideas and fashion (to an extent) cannot be protected. Source

  • Fact 4: Websites

    - You can actually protect entire websites under copyrights as well as other forms of media. Source

  • Fact 5: Rights

    - Copyrights protect the owners product from others who try to do things like perform, reproduce, or incorperate their work without proper permissions. Source
